# Acme Corp — Employee Handbook

## Remote Work Policy

All full-time employees are eligible for remote work up to **3 days per week**.
Employees must be available during core hours: **10:00 AM – 3:00 PM** local time.
A reliable internet connection of at least 25 Mbps is required.

## Vacation & Time Off

- Full-time employees accrue **15 days** of paid time off (PTO) per year.
- PTO increases to **20 days** after 3 years of tenure.
- Unused PTO up to 5 days may be rolled over into the next calendar year.
- Requests must be submitted at least **2 weeks in advance** via the HR portal.

## Expense Reimbursement

Employees may expense the following without prior approval (up to limits shown):

| Category | Limit |
|---|---|
| Home office equipment | $500 / year |
| Internet stipend | $50 / month |
| Professional development | $1,000 / year |
| Meals (client-facing) | $75 / person |

All receipts must be submitted within **30 days** of the expense.

## Code of Conduct

Acme Corp is committed to providing a workplace free from discrimination and harassment.
All employees are expected to treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect.
Violations should be reported to HR at hr@acmecorp.example.com.

## Health Benefits

Medical, dental, and vision coverage begins on the **first day of employment**.
The company covers 80% of premiums for employees and 60% for dependants.
Open enrollment occurs every year in **November**.
